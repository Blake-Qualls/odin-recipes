Salsa

Description

This is a great standard salsa recipe that everyone will love! The coolness of the tomatoes mixed with the sharp flavors of the onion and cilantro are all topped off by a perfect amount of heat from the jalepenos.

Ingredients

  • 4 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tomatillo, diced (Optional)
  • salt to taste
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, minced

Steps

  1. Chop/mince ingredients as stated in the ingredient list
  2. Combine all ingredients into a large bowl and mix well
  3. Cover and chill for at least an hour before serving
