Peanut Butter Fudge

Description

This recipe makes easy yet delicious peanut butter fudge. You will be in shock over how something so tasty was made so simply. With just the right amount of sweetness and a creamy texture this peanut butter fidge will have you dying to make more once it runs out.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 16oz brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Stir in brown sugar and milk bringing it to a boil and letting it go for 2 minutes, stirring frequently.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in peanut butter and vanilla.
  4. Pour over confectioners' sugar in a large mixing bowl and beat until smooth
  5. pour into an 8x8 inch dish and chill until firm
